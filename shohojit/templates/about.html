Our Mission

To provide accessible, high-quality IT education and solutions that empower individuals and businesses to thrive in the digital age. We're committed to bridging the digital skills gap through practical, industry-relevant training and innovative technology services.

Our Vision

To become Bangladesh's leading IT education and solutions provider, recognized for transforming lives and businesses through technology. We envision a future where every individual has access to the digital skills needed to succeed in the global economy.

Our Journey

Milestones in our growth and development

2015

Foundation

Sohoj IT was founded with a vision to provide quality IT education in Dhaka. Started with basic computer courses.

2017

Expansion

Introduced advanced courses in web development and graphic design. Expanded team to 5 instructors.

2019

IT Solutions Launch

Started offering IT consultancy and web development services to businesses alongside training programs.

2021

Digital Transformation

Launched online courses and hybrid learning models to reach students across Bangladesh.

2023

500+ Graduates

Celebrated 500+ successful graduates and expanded our corporate training programs.
